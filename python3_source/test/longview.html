Living at Longview Place

Longview Place Apartments is a beautiful community overlooking the Charles River and downtown Boston. We cater to those who crave an active lifestyle along with convenient access to everything that the modern Boston resident could ask for. Moody Street in Waltham is a very popular dining spot among our residents. Inside, available renovated interiors, upscale kitchens and stunning finishes make you feel right at home. With a movie theater, dog park, fitness center and steam room, there's always a place for our residents to play and thrive. Discover the perfect place to call home here at the incredible Longview Place Apartments.

Waltham

Longview Place in Waltham is located 10 miles from downtown Boston and 3 miles from Brighton. Not only will you find an abundance of history and museums in Waltham, you'll also find live music venues, free community events and a food lover's paradise along Moody Street. Whether you're a graduate student attending one of Waltham's nearby universities or are looking for a cultural hub to raise a family, look no further than Waltham. Visit us today!
